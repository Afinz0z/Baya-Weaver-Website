The Vision

A Sanctuary
Above the Mist

Baya Weaver Resorts is being crafted as Wayanad's most refined eco-luxury destination — a place where indigenous architecture, responsible hospitality, and the raw beauty of the Western Ghats meet in perfect harmony.

Situated in Old Vythiri, just off the National Highway with convenient accessibility, our 3.27-acre estate will host 14 private villas — framed by green land, birdsong, and highland sky.

Address
