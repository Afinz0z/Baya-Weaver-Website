A Sanctuary

Wayanad tourism is among Keralam's fastest-growing segments. Premium eco-luxury villas in Vythiri represent an exceptional long-term asset in a market with very limited comparable supply.

Eco-Responsible Design

Sustainable construction, native landscaping, solar integration, and rainwater harvesting — luxury that takes nothing from the Highland Tea Estate it calls home.

Old Vythiri,

📍

Address

Old Vythiri, Wayanad District, Keralam 673576 — 3.27 acres of Highland Tea Estate on the National Highway corridor

🚗

By Road

~70 km from Calicut (Kozhikode) · ~90 km from Mysore · ~270 km from Bangalore via NH766

✈️

Nearest Airport

Calicut International Airport (CCJ) — approximately 80 km, ~90 minutes by road

🌡️

Elevation & Climate

~800m above sea level. Cool mornings, refreshing highland monsoon, and crisp winter skies year-round

Curated Experiences Nearby

Chembra Peak Wayanad's highest summit — the trek to the heart-shaped lake is iconic
Banasura Sagar Dam India's largest earthen dam, encircled by mist-laden hills
Edakkal Caves Stone-age petroglyphs perched on Ambukuthi Mala — history at altitude
Soochipara Falls A 200ft cascade through rainforest — spectacular after the monsoon
Ennooru Tribal Heritage Village An immersive encounter with indigenous Adivasi culture and traditions
